# Module 14 — Self-Check

Answer from memory, then check yourself against the readings and your notes. If you can't
answer one cleanly, that's the part to revisit.

1. State Conway's Law in one sentence — and then state the *mechanism* that makes it true.
2. What does it mean to say the org is a "system that shapes a system"? Where does the delay
   come in?
3. Define the four Team Topologies team types and give a one-line example of each.
4. Name the three interaction modes. Why is choosing a mode also a Conway decision?
5. What is the Inverse Conway Maneuver, and why is it a higher-leverage move than adding
   coordination meetings?
6. What is a "team API," and how does it relate to the interfaces that will show up in the
   code later?
7. How does **team cognitive load** show up in the *shape* of the architecture a team
   produces?
8. Describe one real homomorphism you found between an org and its architecture — and one
   place the mirror leaked.
