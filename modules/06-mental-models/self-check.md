# Module 06 — Self-Check

Answer from memory, then check yourself against the readings and your notes. If you can't
answer one cleanly, that's the part to revisit.

1. What is a **mental model**, and in what sense does it *filter* what you perceive?
2. List the rungs of the **Ladder of Inference** from bottom to top.
3. What is the **reflexive loop**, and why does it make the ladder self-confirming?
4. Distinguish **advocacy** from **inquiry**. What does balancing them look like in practice?
5. What is the **left-hand column** exercise, and what is it designed to surface?
6. Explain "the map is not the territory" — why is every model incomplete, and why is that
   okay?
7. Describe a recent moment where you climbed the ladder too fast. What data did you skip, and
   what would inquiry have changed?
