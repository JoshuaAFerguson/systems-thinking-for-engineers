# Module 04 — Self-Check

Answer from memory, then check yourself against the readings and your notes. If you can't
answer one cleanly, that's the part to revisit.

1. Why does a delay in a balancing loop produce oscillation instead of a smooth approach to
   the goal?
2. What is the **bullwhip effect**, and what causes it in the Beer Game — the players or the
   structure?
3. State the three ingredients of an **overshoot-and-collapse** pattern.
4. What bends exponential growth into an **S-curve**? Name the loop that takes over.
5. Define **nonlinearity** in one sentence and give an example with a threshold or cliff.
6. What is **bounded rationality**, and why does it mean fixing the *structure* usually beats
   blaming the people?
7. Give a delayed feedback from your own work (autoscaling lag, hiring lag, alert fatigue)
   and describe the oscillation or overshoot it produces.
