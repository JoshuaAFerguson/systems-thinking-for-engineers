# Module 02 — Self-Check

Answer from memory, then check yourself against the readings and your notes. If you can't
answer one cleanly, that's the part to revisit.

1. Define **stock** and **flow** in one sentence each. Which is a noun and which is a verb?
2. Why is a stock called the system's *memory*?
3. State the rule about how you can and can't change a stock. What's the only lever?
4. How does a stock **decouple** its inflow from its outflow, and why is that a source of
   stability?
5. Where do **delays** come from in a stock-and-flow structure?
6. Given a BOTG showing a stock rising and then leveling off, what must be true about its
   inflow and outflow over that time?
7. Give a real stock in your work and name at least one inflow and one outflow for it, with
   correct units.
