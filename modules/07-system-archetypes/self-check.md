# Module 07 — Self-Check

Answer from memory, then check yourself against the readings and your notes. If you can't
answer one cleanly, that's the part to revisit.

1. What *is* an archetype — why is it a structure and not a problem?
2. Name at least six archetypes. For each, give the one-line behavior-over-time it produces.
3. What's the difference between **Fixes that Fail** and **Shifting the Burden**? (Both start
   with a quick fix — what makes them distinct structures?)
4. In **Limits to Growth**, why does pushing harder on the growth engine not help?
5. In **Tragedy of the Commons**, what feature of the feedback (who gets the benefit, who pays
   the cost, and *when*) causes the collapse?
6. **Drift to Low Performance** and **Seeking the Wrong Goal** both involve a goal going wrong.
   How do they differ?
7. Pick any archetype and state its **leverage point** — where you intervene to escape it — and
   why the obvious intervention makes it worse.
8. Which archetype have you personally lived through at work? Name it and the tell.
