# Module 07 — Case Study

> **Your work.** Find an archetype operating in a real system you know — your org, your product,
> your team's process — and name its way out. The goal isn't to force-fit a label; it's to let
> the structure explain a pattern you've been living with.

## The situation

_Describe a recurring, frustrating pattern you've actually seen. What behavior repeats? Over what
timescale?_

## The archetype

_Which archetype is this? Say what made you pick it over its near neighbors._

## The loop structure

_Draw or describe the reinforcing and balancing loops. Mark the delay that does the damage. (An
image, an ASCII sketch, or a LOOPY link all work.)_

## Why the obvious fix fails

_What has someone already tried — the "push harder" move — and how did the structure defeat it?_

## The way out

_Name the higher-leverage intervention this archetype points to. What would it take to actually
do it here, and who would have to act?_

## What I noticed

_Two or three sentences: what did naming the archetype let you see that the day-to-day view
didn't?_
