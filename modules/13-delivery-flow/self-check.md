# Module 13 — Self-Check

Answer from memory, then check yourself against the readings and your notes. If you can't
answer one cleanly, that's the part to revisit.

1. Why is a queue an **invisible stock**? Name three queues in a delivery pipeline.
2. State **Little's Law** and rearrange it to solve for lead time.
3. If WIP doubles and throughput holds, what happens to lead time — and why is that not opinion?
4. What is a **constraint**, and why does improving a non-constraint step produce no system
   improvement?
5. List the five focusing steps of the **Theory of Constraints**, in order.
6. Give two reasons **high WIP** is a false economy.
7. Why does **large batch size** lengthen lead time and delay feedback?
8. Why is a **WIP limit** a leverage point even though it makes no one work faster?
